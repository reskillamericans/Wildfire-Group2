About Fire Alert

Fire Alert was created by web development students from ground up to track wildfires, notify you of updates, and provide resources for those in the affected areas. You can find shelter, update your status and let people know you are safe and if you need anything. Many of the features are still in development but there are more exciting things to come soon.

Try it for free today!

Why Are We Doing This

Fire Alert was developed as a class project by the students of the Reskill Americans software development program. The app was designed to track wildfires along the west coast of the United States where wildfires are common. It will keep you up to date on the current ongoing fires and you can alert others of your status and let them know if you are safe or need help.

Reskill Americans is a nonprofit that provides tuition-free software development training, mentorship, and community to historically underrepresented racial minorities across the U.S. We eliminate traditional barriers to access so that aspiring tech professionals with no prior experience can enroll in our rigorous, instructor-led online program. Participants who complete our seven-month program will be set on a path for a career in technology, equipped with internship experience, interview skills, and a portfolio of work to show future employers.
